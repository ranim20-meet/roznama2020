Who are we?

We are a small group of students from MEET who aim to help those with time management issues caused by mostly their mental state. We decided to tackle the issue of time mangement from a different direction than other time management apps as we realized most of them help for the first week and then become obnoxious or they are simply expensive.

The path we took, was targeting a cycle that starts with lack of time management and causes lack of motivation which encourages lack of time management.
We focus mainly on your mental health, so that you can perform your task as effeciently as possible.
